*For: MBBS students, nurses, paramedics, health workers, patients*

- Treatment: Therapy, Counseling, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Medication, Meditation, Support group
- Terms: Psychologist, Psychiatrist, Therapist, Mental wellness, Self-care, Mindfulness, Resilience, Stigma

### C-01f. Medical Equipment

- Treatment: IV drip, Ventilator, Oxygen cylinder, Nebulizer, Defibrillator, Wheelchair, Crutches
