1. Grammar Error Correction System

(व्याकरण त्रुटि सुधार प्रणाली)


---

Example 1

❌ He do not play cricket.

✅ He does not play cricket.


---

Why Wrong?

He/she/it के साथ: does उपयोग होता है।


---

Example 2

❌ She go to school daily.

✅ She goes to school daily.


---

Example 3

❌ I am study English.

✅ I am studying English.


---

Why Wrong?

am/is/are के बाद: V4 आता है।


---

Example 4

❌ They was playing football.

✅ They were playing football.

3. Natural English Upgrade System

(प्राकृतिक अंग्रेज़ी उन्नयन प्रणाली)


---

Example 1

⚠️ I drink tea.

✅ I have tea.
✅ I usually have tea in the evening.


---

Important Understanding

“I drink tea” गलत नहीं है।

लेकिन: “I have tea” daily natural conversation में ज्यादा common है।


---

Example 2

⚠️ I am very tired.

✅ I am exhausted.


---

Example 3

⚠️ He is very angry.

✅ He is furious.


---

Important Understanding

Natural English: कई बार छोटे simple words की जगह strong natural expressions use करती है।

6. Verb Usage Error System

(क्रिया प्रयोग त्रुटि प्रणाली)


---

Learn vs Study


---

Wrong Understanding

❌ Both are same.


---

Correct Understanding

Learn
→ skill development

Study
→ subject reading


---

Example

I am learning English.
मैं English सीख रहा हूँ।

I am studying English grammar.
मैं English grammar पढ़ रहा हूँ।


---

Drink vs Have vs Take Tea


---

I drink tea.

✅ Modern global English


---

I have tea.

✅ Most natural daily English


---

I take tea.

⚠️ formal/traditional/British-style usage


---

Important Understanding

English verbs: Hindi physical logic से नहीं, native usage pattern से चलते हैं।


---

Hear vs Listen

hear
→ automatic hearing

listen
→ attention-based hearing


---

See vs Look vs Watch

see
→ naturally visible

look
→ intentionally see

watch
→ continuously observe
