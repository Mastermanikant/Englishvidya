What is an Adverb?

English Definition

The word that describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb is called an adverb.


---

Hindi Definition

वह शब्द जो किसी verb, adjective या दूसरे adverb की विशेषता बताए, उसे adverb कहते हैं।


---

Important Understanding

Adjective

noun/pronoun को modify करता है।


---

Adverb

verb

adjective

दूसरे adverb


को modify करता है।


---

1. Adverb Modifying a Verb

Example

He runs quickly.


---

Explanation

runs
→ verb

quickly
→ runs को modify कर रहा है।

इसलिए: quickly = adverb


---

More Examples

She sings beautifully.
→ वह सुंदर गाती है।

They worked hard.
→ उन्होंने मेहनत से काम किया।


---

Important Understanding

यहाँ adverb: काम कैसे हुआ, यह बता रहा है।


---

2. Adverb Modifying an Adjective

Example

He is very smart.


---

Explanation

smart
→ adjective

very
→ smart को modify कर रहा है।

इसलिए: very = adverb


---

More Examples

She is extremely beautiful.

This question is too difficult.


---

Important Understanding

यहाँ adverb: adjective की intensity बढ़ा रहा है।


---

3. Adverb Modifying Another Adverb

Example

He runs very quickly.


---

Explanation

quickly
→ adverb

very
→ quickly को modify कर रहा है।

इसलिए: very भी adverb है।


---

More Examples

She speaks quite softly.

They worked very carefully.


---

Final Easy Rule

Verb को modify करे

→ adverb


---

Adjective को modify करे

→ adverb


---

दूसरे adverb को modify करे

→ adverb


---

Examples

---

Important Understanding

लेकिन: सभी adverbs “ly” वाले नहीं होते।

Examples

Types of Adverb

अब हम adverb के मुख्य प्रकार समझेंगे।


---

1. Adverb of Manner

(रीतिवाचक क्रिया-विशेषण)

English Definition

The adverb that tells how an action happens is called an adverb of manner.


---

Hindi Definition

जो adverb यह बताए कि कार्य कैसे हुआ, उसे Adverb of Manner कहते हैं।


---

Examples

slowly
quickly
carefully
beautifully


---

Example Sentences

The boy writes neatly.

She danced beautifully.


---

Question Trick

How?


---

Example

How does he write?

Answer: neatly


---

2. Adverb of Time

(कालवाचक क्रिया-विशेषण)

English Definition

The adverb that tells when an action happens is called an adverb of time.


---

Hindi Definition

जो adverb यह बताए कि कार्य कब हुआ, उसे Adverb of Time कहते हैं।


---

Examples

today
tomorrow
yesterday
now


---

Example Sentences

We will go tomorrow.

He came yesterday.


---

Question Trick

When?


---

Example

When will we go?

Answer: tomorrow


---

3. Adverb of Place

(स्थानवाचक क्रिया-विशेषण)

English Definition

The adverb that tells where an action happens is called an adverb of place.


---

Hindi Definition

जो adverb यह बताए कि कार्य कहाँ हुआ, उसे Adverb of Place कहते हैं।


---

Examples

here
there
outside
upstairs


---

Example Sentences

Please sit here.

The cat slept upstairs.


---

Question Trick

Where?


---

Example

Where did the cat sleep?

Answer: upstairs


---

4. Adverb of Frequency

(आवृत्तिवाचक क्रिया-विशेषण)

English Definition

The adverb that tells how often an action happens is called an adverb of frequency.


---

Hindi Definition

जो adverb यह बताए कि कार्य कितनी बार होता है, उसे Adverb of Frequency कहते हैं।


---

Examples

always
often
sometimes
never


---

Example Sentences

He always speaks the truth.

She never drinks tea.


---

Question Trick

How often?


---

5. Adverb of Degree

(परिमाणवाचक क्रिया-विशेषण)

English Definition

The adverb that tells the intensity or degree of something is called an adverb of degree.


---

Hindi Definition

जो adverb किसी कार्य, गुण या अवस्था की मात्रा या तीव्रता बताए, उसे Adverb of Degree कहते हैं।


---

Examples

very
too
quite
extremely


---

Example Sentences

She is very smart.

This box is too heavy.


---

Adverb Position

Adverb sentence में अलग-अलग स्थान पर आ सकता है।


---

Beginning

Yesterday, I met him.


---

Middle

He always comes late.


---

End

She sings beautifully.


---

Important Understanding

Adverb की position: उसके type और emphasis पर depend करती है।

Multiple Adverbs in One Sentence

Example

The tired cat slept quietly upstairs.


---

Explanation

quietly
→ कैसे सोया?

upstairs

---

Important Understanding

एक sentence में: एक से अधिक adverbs हो सकते हैं।
