What is a Preposition?

English Definition

A word placed before a noun, pronoun, or noun phrase to show its relation with another part of the sentence is called a preposition.


---

Hindi Definition

वह शब्द जो noun, pronoun या noun phrase से पहले आकर उसका संबंध sentence के दूसरे शब्दों से जोड़ता है, preposition कहलाता है।


---

Important Understanding

Preposition: दो words या groups के बीच relation दिखाता है।


---

Examples

in
on
at
to
with
under
over
beside
between


---

Example Sentences

The book is on the table.

Ram is in the room.

She sat beside me.


---

Relation Logic

Preposition सामान्यतः बताता है:

कहाँ?

कब?

किस दिशा में?

किसके साथ?

किसके ऊपर/नीचे?

Types of Preposition

अब हम preposition के मुख्य प्रकार समझेंगे।

1. Preposition of Place

(स्थान बोधक संबंध)

English Definition

The preposition that shows place or position is called a preposition of place.


---

Hindi Definition

जो preposition किसी स्थान या स्थिति का बोध कराए, उसे Preposition of Place कहते हैं।


---

Examples

in
on
under
behind
beside


---

Example Sentences

The cat is under the table.

The picture is on the wall.

Ram is in the classroom.


---

Important Understanding

in

अंदर


---

on

ऊपर (touching surface)


---

under

नीचे

2. Preposition of Time

(समय बोधक संबंध)

English Definition

The preposition that shows time is called a preposition of time.


---

Hindi Definition

जो preposition समय का बोध कराए, उसे Preposition of Time कहते हैं।


---

Main Time Prepositions

at
on

---

Use of “At”

Specific time


---

Examples

---

Use of “On”

Specific day/date


---

Examples

---

Use of “In”

Longer periods

Examples

---

Final Easy Rule

exact time


---

On

day/date


---

In

3. Preposition of Direction

(दिशा बोधक संबंध)

English Definition

The preposition that shows movement or direction is called a preposition of direction.


---

Hindi Definition

जो preposition दिशा या movement का बोध कराए, उसे Preposition of Direction कहते हैं।


---

Examples

to
towards
into
onto


---

Example Sentences

He went to school.

The boy jumped into the river.

She walked towards the market.


---

Important Understanding

Direction prepositions: movement दिखाते हैं।

4. Preposition of Cause or Purpose

(कारण/उद्देश्य बोधक संबंध)

English Definition

The preposition that shows reason or purpose is called a preposition of cause or purpose.


---

Hindi Definition

जो preposition कारण या उद्देश्य का बोध कराए, उसे Preposition of Cause/Purpose कहते हैं।


---

Examples

---

Example Sentences

He was punished for his mistake.

The match was cancelled because of rain.

Preposition vs Adverb

कई बार same word:

preposition या

adverb


दोनों हो सकता है।


---

Example 1

He went inside the room.

inside + noun
→ preposition


---

Example 2

He went inside.

noun नहीं है
→ adverb


---

Important Understanding

Preposition

के बाद object आता है।


---

Adverb

के बाद object जरूरी नहीं।


---

Important Preposition Difference

In vs Into

In

स्थिति

The boy is in the room.


---

Into

movement अंदर की ओर

The boy went into the room.


---

On vs Onto

On

स्थिति

The book is on the table.


---

Onto

movement ऊपर की ओर

The cat jumped onto the table.


---

Between vs Among

Between

दो के बीच

The ball is between Ram and Mohan.


---

Among

कई लोगों/वस्तुओं के बीच

He was sitting among friends.


---

Common Mistakes

❌ He discussed about the issue.
✅ He discussed the issue.


---

❌ She entered into the room.
✅ She entered the room.

---

Prepositional Phrase

English Definition

A group of words beginning with a preposition and ending with its object is called a prepositional phrase.


---

Hindi Definition

शब्दों का वह समूह जो preposition से शुरू होकर उसके object पर समाप्त हो, prepositional phrase कहलाता है।


---

Examples

in the room
under the table
beside the river
