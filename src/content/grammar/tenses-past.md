# Past Tense (भूतकाल)

**English:** The past tense is used to describe an action that has already happened.

**Hindi:** भूतकाल उस कार्य को दर्शाता है जो बीते समय में हो चुका है।

## Simple Past
Completed actions

- I played cricket. (मैंने क्रिकेट खेला।)
- She went to school. (वह स्कूल गई।)

## Past Continuous
Action happening in the past

- I was eating. (मैं खा रहा था।)
- They were playing. (वे खेल रहे थे।)
