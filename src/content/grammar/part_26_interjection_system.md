---

What is an Interjection?

English Definition

A word or phrase used to express sudden emotion without adding grammatical meaning to the sentence is called an interjection.


---

Hindi Definition

वह शब्द या शब्द समूह जो अचानक उत्पन्न भावना या आवेग को व्यक्त करे, लेकिन sentence में कोई grammatical meaning न जोड़े, interjection कहलाता है।


---

Important Understanding

Interjection: grammar structure का मुख्य भाग नहीं होता।

यह: speaker की feeling/emotion दिखाता है।

joy

---

1. Interjection of Joy

(खुशी)

Examples

Hurray!
Hurrah!
Wow!


---

Example Sentences

Hurray! We won the match.

Wow! This is amazing.


---

Explanation

यहाँ: interjection अचानक खुशी व्यक्त कर रहा है।


---

2. Interjection of Surprise

(आश्चर्य)

Examples

Oh!
Wow!
What!


---

Example Sentences

Wow! What a beautiful house!

Oh! I did not know that.


---

Explanation

यहाँ: speaker की surprise दिखाई जा रही है।


---

3. Interjection of Pain

(दर्द)

Examples

Ouch!
Ah!
Alas!


---

Example Sentences

Ouch! The pin pricked me.

Ah! My leg hurts.


---

Explanation

यहाँ: interjection अचानक दर्द व्यक्त कर रहा है।


---

4. Interjection of Sorrow

(दुःख)

Examples

Alas!
Oh no!


---

Example Sentences

Alas! He is dead.

Oh no! We lost the game.


---

Explanation

यहाँ: दुःख या अफसोस व्यक्त हो रहा है।


---

5. Interjection of Greeting

(अभिवादन)

Examples

Hello!
Hi!
Good morning!


---

Example Sentences

Hello! How are you?

Good morning! Students.


---

6. Interjection of Approval

(प्रशंसा/स्वीकृति)

Examples

Bravo!
Excellent!


---

Example Sentences

Bravo! You did well.

Excellent! Your answer is correct.


---

Important Understanding

Interjection: sentence के बाकी grammar structure से अलग भी खड़ा हो सकता है।


---

Examples

Wow!
Amazing!
Oops!


---

Interjection and Punctuation

Interjection के बाद सामान्यतः: exclamation mark (!)

लगाया जाता है।


---

Examples

Hurray!
Ouch!
Wow!


---

Important Understanding

Exclamation mark: strong emotion दिखाता है।


---

Interjection vs Normal Sentence

Interjection

Wow!


---

Normal Sentence

This house is very beautiful.


---

Difference

Interjection: emotion directly व्यक्त करता है।

Normal sentence: complete information देता है।


---

Interjection in Spoken English

Spoken English में: interjections communication को:

natural

expressive

emotional


बनाते हैं।


---

Examples

Oops! I forgot my book.

Hey! Listen to me.

Well! Let us start.


---

Common Mistakes

❌ Hurray we won the match
✅ Hurray! We won the match.


---

❌ Wow what a beautiful place
✅ Wow! What a beautiful place!


---

Important Understanding

Interjection: sentence में meaning add नहीं करता, लेकिन emotion add करता है।
