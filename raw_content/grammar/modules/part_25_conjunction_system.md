What is a Conjunction?

English Definition

The word that is used to connect words, phrases, or clauses is called a conjunction.


---

Hindi Definition

वह शब्द जो दो शब्दों, वाक्यांशों या उपवाक्यों को जोड़ने का कार्य करे, conjunction कहलाता है।


---

Important Understanding

Conjunction: एक bond या connection की तरह काम करता है।


---

Connecting Words

tea and coffee

Ram and Shyam


---

Connecting Phrases

on the table or under the table


---

Connecting Clauses

I wanted to go, but it was raining.


---

Types of Conjunction

अब हम conjunction के मुख्य प्रकार समझेंगे।


---

1. Coordinating Conjunction

(समन्वयी समुच्चयबोधक)

English Definition

The conjunction that joins words, phrases, or clauses of equal importance is called a coordinating conjunction.


---

Hindi Definition

जो conjunction समान महत्व वाले words, phrases या clauses को जोड़े, उसे Coordinating Conjunction कहते हैं।


---

Main Coordinating Conjunctions

and
but
or
so
for
nor
yet


---

Functions of Coordinating Conjunction

Addition

and


---

Examples

Ram and Shyam

Tea and coffee


---

Contrast

but / yet


---

Examples

He is rich but unhappy.

She is wise yet humble.


---

Choice

or


---

Examples

Tea or coffee

You can stay or leave.


---

Result

so


---

Examples

It was raining, so we stayed home.


---

Important Understanding

Coordinating conjunction: दो equal level के parts को जोड़ता है।


---

2. Subordinating Conjunction

(आश्रित समुच्चयबोधक)

English Definition

The conjunction that joins a dependent clause to an independent clause is called a subordinating conjunction.


---

Hindi Definition

जो conjunction dependent clause को independent clause से जोड़े, उसे Subordinating Conjunction कहते हैं।


---

Examples

because
if
when
although
since
while


---

Example Sentences

I stayed home because it was raining.

When the teacher came, the students stood up.


---

Clause Understanding

Independent Clause

जो अकेले पूरा meaning दे सके।

Example:

I stayed home.


---

Dependent Clause

जो अकेले पूरा meaning न दे सके।

Example:

because it was raining


---

Important Understanding

Subordinating conjunction: एक clause को दूसरे पर dependent बनाता है।


---

3. Correlative Conjunction

(सहसंबंधी समुच्चयबोधक)

English Definition

The conjunctions that work in pairs are called correlative conjunctions.


---

Hindi Definition

जो conjunction जोड़े में कार्य करें, उन्हें Correlative Conjunction कहते हैं।


---

Examples

either…or
neither…nor
both…and
not only…but also


---

Example Sentences

Either Ram or Mohan will come.

Neither the teacher nor the students were ready.

Both tea and coffee are available.


---

Important Understanding

Correlative conjunction: pair में काम करता है।


---

Conjunction vs Preposition

Conjunction

clauses/words जोड़ता है।

Example:

I was tired but I worked.


---

Preposition

noun/pronoun से relation दिखाता है।

Example:

He sat on the chair.


---

Conjunction vs Relative Pronoun

Relative Pronoun

noun + clause जोड़ता है।

Example:

The boy who came here is my friend.


---

Conjunction

दो clauses जोड़ता है।

Example:

I came because you called me.


---

Important Understanding

Relative pronoun: noun का relation बनाता है।

Conjunction: clause connection बनाता है।

Common Mistakes

❌ Because he was ill.
→ incomplete sentence

✅ He stayed home because he was ill.


---

❌ Although he is rich but unhappy.
✅ Although he is rich, he is unhappy.
✅ He is rich but unhappy.
