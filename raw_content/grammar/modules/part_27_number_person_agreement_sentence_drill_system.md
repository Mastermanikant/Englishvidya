Number System

(वचन प्रणाली)

English Definition

The counting of things is called number.


---

Hindi Definition

वस्तुओं की गिनती को number या वचन कहते हैं।


---

Types of Number

1. Singular Number


2. Plural Number




---

Singular Number

(एकवचन)

English Definition

The noun that refers to one person, place, thing, idea, or quality is called singular number.


---

Hindi Definition

जो noun एक व्यक्ति, स्थान, वस्तु, विचार या गुण का बोध कराए, उसे singular number कहते हैं।


---

Examples

boy
girl
book
apple


---

Plural Number

(बहुवचन)

English Definition

The noun that refers to more than one person, place, thing, idea, or quality is called plural number.


---

Hindi Definition

जो noun एक से अधिक व्यक्ति, स्थान, वस्तु, विचार या गुण का बोध कराए, उसे plural number कहते हैं।


---

Examples

boys
girls
books
apples

Subject-Verb Agreement

(कर्ता-क्रिया सामंजस्य)

English Definition

The verb must agree with its subject in number and person.


---

Hindi Definition

Verb को अपने subject के number और person के अनुसार बदलना पड़ता है।


---

Present Form Agreement

SubjectVerb

I/We/You/Theygo
He/She/Itgoes



---

Examples

I go to school.
He goes to school.

We play cricket.
She plays cricket.


---

Past Form Agreement

Past form सामान्यतः सभी subjects के साथ same रहती है।


---

Examples

I went to school.
He went to school.
They went to school.


---

---

Past Form

Examples

---

Future Form

Sentence Forms

(वाक्य के प्रकार)


---

1. Affirmative Sentence

(सकारात्मक वाक्य)

English Definition

A sentence that makes a positive statement is called an affirmative sentence.


---

Hindi Definition

जो वाक्य सामान्य सकारात्मक कथन दे, उसे affirmative sentence कहते हैं।


---

Examples

I am happy.
He plays cricket.
They are students.


---

2. Negative Sentence

(नकारात्मक वाक्य)

English Definition

A sentence that expresses negation is called a negative sentence.


---

Hindi Definition

जो वाक्य निषेध या इंकार व्यक्त करे, उसे negative sentence कहते हैं।


---

Examples

I am not happy.
He does not play cricket.
They are not students.


---

3. Interrogative Sentence

(प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य)

English Definition

A sentence that asks a question is called an interrogative sentence.


---

Hindi Definition

जो वाक्य प्रश्न पूछे, उसे interrogative sentence कहते हैं।


---

Examples

Am I happy?
Does he play cricket?
Are they students?


---

4. Interrogative Negative Sentence

(नकारात्मक प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य)

English Definition

A sentence that is both interrogative and negative is called an interrogative negative sentence.


---

Hindi Definition

जो वाक्य प्रश्न और निषेध दोनों व्यक्त करे, उसे interrogative negative sentence कहते हैं।


---

Examples

Am I not happy?
Does he not play cricket?
Are they not students?


---

Short Answer System

Examples

Are you ready?
→ Yes, I am.
→ No, I am not.


---

Is he happy?
→ Yes, he is.
→ No, he is not.


---

Contracted Forms

(Short Spoken Forms)

Examples

I am → I’m
He is → He’s
They are → They’re

do not → don’t
does not → doesn’t

is not → isn’t
are not → aren’t


---

Important Understanding

Contracted forms: spoken English को natural बनाते हैं।
