What is a Verb?

English Definition

The word that expresses an action or a state of being is called a verb.


---

Hindi Definition

वह शब्द जो किसी कार्य, अवस्था या घटना को बताए, verb कहलाता है।


---

Examples

run → दौड़ना
eat → खाना
write → लिखना
think → सोचना


---

Important Understanding

Verb केवल physical action नहीं बताता।

यह:

मानसिक क्रिया

अवस्था

अस्तित्व


भी बता सकता है।

1. Action Verb

---

1. Action Verb

(कार्यवाचक क्रिया)

English Definition

The verb that expresses an action is called an action verb.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb किसी कार्य या क्रिया का बोध कराए, उसे Action Verb कहते हैं।


---

Physical Actions

run → दौड़ना
eat → खाना
beat → मारना
write → लिखना


---

Mental Actions

think → सोचना
understand → समझना
remember → याद रखना
believe → विश्वास करना


---

Example Sentences

He runs fast.
→ वह तेज दौड़ता है।

I understand the lesson.
→ मैं पाठ समझता हूँ।


---

2. State Verb

(अवस्थावाचक क्रिया)

English Definition

The verb that expresses a state or condition is called a state verb.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb किसी अवस्था या स्थिति का बोध कराए, उसे State Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

am
is
are
was
were


---

Example Sentences

He is a boy.
→ वह लड़का है।

She is happy.
→ वह खुश है।

I am a teacher.
→ मैं शिक्षक हूँ।


---

Important Understanding

इन sentences में: कोई action नहीं हो रहा।

Verb: स्थिति/अवस्था बता रहा है।


---

Main Verb

(मुख्य क्रिया)

English Definition

The main verb is the principal verb that expresses the main action or state in a sentence.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb sentence का मुख्य कार्य या मुख्य अवस्था बताए, उसे Main Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

He plays cricket.

यहाँ: plays = main verb


---

She is singing.

यहाँ: singing = main verb


---

Helping Verb / Auxiliary Verb

(सहायक क्रिया)

English Definition

The verb that helps the main verb to form tense, voice, mood, or question is called a helping verb or auxiliary verb.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb मुख्य क्रिया की सहायता करे, उसे Helping Verb या Auxiliary Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

am
is
are
was
were
do
does
did
have
has
had
will
shall


---

Example Sentences

She is singing.

is → helping verb
singing → main verb


---

I have completed my work.

have → helping verb
completed → main verb


---

Important Understanding

Helping verb:

tense बनाने में

question बनाने में

negative sentence बनाने में


मदद करता है।

Finite Verb

(निश्चित क्रिया)

English Definition

A finite verb is a verb that changes according to tense, number, or person.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb tense, number या person के अनुसार बदलती है, उसे Finite Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

He goes to school.

They go to school.


---

Important Understanding

यहाँ: go/goes

subject के अनुसार बदल रहा है।

इसलिए: finite verb


---

Non-Finite Verb

(अनिश्चित क्रिया)

English Definition

A non-finite verb is a verb that does not change according to tense, number, or person.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb tense, number या person के अनुसार नहीं बदलती, उसे Non-Finite Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

To read is good.

Reading is useful.

He likes to swim.


---

Important Understanding

यहाँ: to read
reading
to swim

subject या tense के अनुसार नहीं बदल रहे।


---

Transitive Verb

(सकर्मक क्रिया)

English Definition

The verb that requires an object to complete its meaning is called a transitive verb.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb अपना अर्थ पूरा करने के लिए object माँगे, उसे Transitive Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

He eats mangoes.


---

Explanation

eats → verb
mangoes → object


---

Important Understanding

अगर object हटाएँ:

He eats…

sentence अधूरा लगता है।


---

Intransitive Verb

(अकर्मक क्रिया)

English Definition

The verb that does not require an object to complete its meaning is called an intransitive verb.


---

Hindi Definition

जो verb अपना अर्थ पूरा करने के लिए object न माँगे, उसे Intransitive Verb कहते हैं।


---

Examples

The baby cried.

Birds fly.


---

Important Understanding

यहाँ: कोई object आवश्यक नहीं है।


---

Subject-Verb Agreement

Verb को: subject के number और person के अनुसार बदलना पड़ता है।


---

Examples

He goes to school. ✅

They go to school. ✅


---

He go to school. ❌


---

Important Understanding

Singular Subject

→ singular verb


---

Plural Subject

→ plural verb
