What is Tense?

English Definition

A tense is a form of verb that shows the time of an action or state.


---

Hindi Definition

Verb का वह रूप जो किसी कार्य या अवस्था का समय बताए, tense कहलाता है।

1. Present Indefinite Tense

(Simple Present)

Main Use

daily habit

routine

universal truth

repeated action



---

Structure

Affirmative

Subject + V1/V5 + Object


---

Examples

I play cricket.
मैं क्रिकेट खेलता हूँ।

He goes to school.
वह स्कूल जाता है।

The sun rises in the east.
सूर्य पूर्व में उगता है।


---

Negative Structure

Subject + do/does + not + V1


---

Examples

I do not play cricket.

He does not go to school.


---

Interrogative Structure

Do/Does + Subject + V1?


---

Examples

Do you play cricket?

Does he go to school?


---

WH Structure

WH + do/does + subject + V1?


---

Examples

Why do you study?

Where does he live?


---

Important Agreement Rule

He/She/It

→ V5


---

Examples

He plays.

She writes.


---

But

I play.

They play.


---

2. Present Continuous Tense

Main Use

जो काम अभी इस समय चल रहा हो।


---

Structure

Subject + am/is/are + V4


---

Examples

I am reading.

She is singing.

They are playing.


---

Negative

Subject + am/is/are + not + V4


---

Examples

I am not reading.

He is not sleeping.


---

Interrogative

Am/Is/Are + Subject + V4?


---

Examples

Are you reading?

Is she dancing?


---

WH Pattern

What are you doing?

Why is he crying?


---

Important Understanding

Continuous tense: चल रहे action को दिखाता है।


---

3. Past Indefinite Tense

Main Use

बीते समय में पूरा हुआ कार्य।


---

Structure

Subject + V2


---

Examples

I went home.

She wrote a letter.

They played football.


---

Negative

Subject + did not + V1


---

Examples

I did not go.

He did not play.


---

Interrogative

Did + Subject + V1?


---

Examples

Did you come?

Did she sing?


---

Important Rule

Did के बाद: हमेशा V1 आएगा।


---

Wrong vs Right

❌ Did he went?
✅ Did he go?


---

4. Future Indefinite Tense

Main Use

भविष्य का कार्य।


---

Structure

Subject + shall/will + V1


---

Examples

I shall go.

He will play.

They will come.


---

Negative

Subject + shall/will + not + V1


---

Examples

I shall not go.

She will not sing.


---

Interrogative

Shall/Will + Subject + V1?


---

Examples

Will you come?

Shall I help you?


---

5. Present Perfect Tense

Main Use

जो कार्य अभी-अभी पूरा हुआ हो या जिसका प्रभाव वर्तमान में हो।


---

Structure

Subject + has/have + V3


---

Examples

I have finished my work.

She has written a letter.

They have gone home.


---

Negative

Subject + has/have + not + V3


---

Examples

He has not eaten food.

They have not arrived.


---

Interrogative

Has/Have + Subject + V3?


---

Examples

Have you completed the work?

Has she gone home?
